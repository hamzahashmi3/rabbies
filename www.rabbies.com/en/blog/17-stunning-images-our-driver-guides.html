2016 was a little strange.

Politics ventured into the unknown, some of our favourite people took their last bow, and mankind watched a mum wearing a Chewbacca mask eight million times.

But it wasn’t all bad. Because the panda population grew, a solar-powered plane circumnavigated the globe, and volunteers in India planted 50 million trees in 24 hours.
